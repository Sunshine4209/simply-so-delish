Roasted Pepper

I do not know what to say here. this is weird!!!

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 2 zucchinis, julienned cut
  • 2 carrots, julienned cut
  • 1 Tbsp butter, melted
  • 1 Tbsp fresh chopped rosemary
  • or 1 tsp dried
  • 4 slices bacon

Healthy, delicious, and easy to make. These chicken bundle are high in vitamin A, protein, and low carb perfect for you paleo fan.

Directions:
  1. In a large bowl, mix cut zucchinis, carrots, melted butter, salt and pepper, and rosemary. Mix throughly and let it sit for about 10 minutes.

  2. in the meantime, preheat oven to 375 F. Using a kitchen hammer and wax paper or a zip-block bag, flatten chicken breast to about 1/4 to 1/3 inch thick.

  3. Drizzle olive oil at the bottom of a baking dish.

  4. To assemble chicken bundles: take 1/4 of vegetables mixture and place in in center on flatten chicken. Roll chicken breast and wrap chicken with a slice of bacon. Use tooth pick to keep it together. Place in baking dish. Repeat with the rest of the vegetables, chicken, and bacon.

  5. Bake for about 35 minutes or until chicken is throughly cooked.

Bon Appétit!
